Token List

Design tokens are tiny pieces of UI information that are used to ensure brand consistency and product design-engineer collaboration across all properties.

⚠️ Design tokens and theming is still a work in progress. Check out the roadmap to learn more.

Breakpoints

Match takes a mobile-first approach to responsive web design. These breakpoints provide ranges needed to ensure that your UI communicates valuable information for customers of all screen sizes.

TokenValueExample
breakpoint.smallscreen and (min-width: 360px)
breakpoint.mediumscreen and (min-width: 760px)
breakpoint.largescreen and (min-width: 1024px)
breakpoint.xLargescreen and (min-width: 1200px)

Colors

Primary

This palette defines our brand. Emphasize Twilio Red and avoid introducing too many secondary colors for audiences new to Twilio.
